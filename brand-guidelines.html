02 — Logo

The Kelker Mark

Primary — On White / Light
Reversed — On Kelker Green
Reversed — On Dark

Logo Anatomy

  • A

    Leaf Icon

    The leaf rising from the "i" represents natural origins, vitality, and growth. It anchors the brand in organic health while signalling pharmaceutical precision.

  • B

    KPI Lettermark

    Lowercase "kpi" in a clean geometric sans. The lowercase treatment conveys approachability; the bold weight communicates strength.

  • C

    Rule Line

    A precise horizontal rule in Kelker Green separates mark from wordmark — creating visual hierarchy and deliberate balance.

  • D

    KELKER Wordmark

    Wide-tracked uppercase "KELKER" in Kelker Green. Spaced caps reinforce authority, transparency, and precision.

Clear Space

1× cap-height of "K" on all sides

Always maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the cap-height of the "K" in KELKER on all four sides. This protected zone must remain free of text, imagery, and graphic elements.

This rule applies regardless of scale, background, or medium. In practice, when in doubt, give the logo more room — never less.

Parchment #F2EEE6 Alternate section background

Usage Principles

Dominance

Kelker Green should represent 60–70% of the color presence in any composition. The palette should feel grown from the green — never competing with it.

Contrast

Maintain a minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for all body text. Never place Kelker Green text on colored backgrounds without verifying accessibility compliance.

Warmth

Prefer Warm White (#FAFAF5) and Parchment (#F2EEE6) over stark white. These tones soften the brand while preserving its clean, precise character.

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

01 — Voice Pillar

Confident, Not Arrogant

We speak with expertise grounded in decades of combined industry experience and millions of units produced. We do not boast — we demonstrate through track record, certifications, and precision.

02 — Voice Pillar

Approachable, Not Casual

Partners come to us with important products and high stakes. We treat every inquiry with warmth and respect, while maintaining the professional rigor a cGMP-certified manufacturer demands.

03 — Voice Pillar

Direct, Not Blunt

We respect our partners' time. Lead with the answer; support it with evidence. Efficiency in language mirrors efficiency in manufacturing. No filler, no hedging, no ambiguity.

Science-Led, Human-First

Accuracy matters deeply. We back every claim with data, certifications, and expertise — but we communicate in plain language, never burying partners in jargon when clarity serves them better.

06 — Photography

Visual Language

01 — Category

Facility & Process

Showcase the precision of our manufacturing environment. Clean, bright, and clinical — never cold. Capture machinery in motion, technicians at work, and the scale of our 75,000 sq ft operation. Wide-angle establishing shots paired with tight detail photos.

Bright Clinical In Motion Wide + Detail
02 — Category

Product & Packaging

Finished product photographed against warm-white or neutral backgrounds. Natural or diffused light preferred. Minimal retouching. Emphasize quality through macro detail — capsules, tablets, powders, bottles. Textures of raw ingredients are brand-authentic and encouraged.

Natural Light Macro Minimal Styling Texture
03 — Category

People & Team

Candid and authentic over staged. Our team reflects expertise, care, and diversity — capture them in context, in conversation, at work. Real people, real moments, real facility. Avoid generic stock photography that could belong to any company.

Authentic In Context Candid Diverse
Image Direction
  • ✓ Clean, bright environments with natural or softbox light
  • ✓ Slight warm lift + 10% desaturation for consistency
  • ✓ Personnel in proper PPE and professional attire
  • ✓ Warm-white or neutral backgrounds for product shots
  • ✓ Visible textures: powders, capsules, herbs, botanicals
What To Avoid
  • ✗ Generic stock: smiling people pointing at charts
  • ✗ Dark, moody, or heavily color-graded imagery
  • ✗ Cluttered, untidy, or unsafe-looking environments
  • ✗ Oversaturated or artificially vivid treatments
  • ✗ Competitor product references or unlicensed images

07 — Certifications

Quality Marks

Kelker Pharma's certifications are a cornerstone of our brand promise and our partners' confidence. Always display them accurately and in their approved form. Never modify, disproportionately resize, or editorialize certification marks without guidance from the issuing body.

🏭

cGMP

Current Good Manufacturing Practices — FDA-regulated production standards governing facility, process, and quality control.

🇺🇸

FDA Registered

Registered facility with the U.S. Food & Drug Administration. Compliance with 21 CFR standards.

🌿

USDA Organic

Certified organic production under the USDA National Organic Program for qualifying product lines.

✡

Kosher

Certified Kosher manufacturing standards maintained for applicable product lines and formulations.

☪

Halal

Certified Halal production processes for applicable product lines in compliance with Islamic dietary law.

⭐

NSF

National Sanitation Foundation certification validating quality, safety, and manufacturing integrity.

🌱

Oregon Tilth

Organic certification from Oregon Tilth Certified Organic (OTCO), one of the most respected certifiers in North America.

"Quality isn't a department. It's a standard."

Usage Note

Certification marks are owned by their respective issuing bodies and are subject to their individual usage guidelines. Always obtain official marks directly from the certifying organization. All external uses of certification marks in communications, packaging, or marketing materials must be reviewed by Kelker Pharma's compliance and legal team prior to publication.
