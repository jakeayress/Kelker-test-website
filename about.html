About Kelker Pharma

Where Life
Begins®

Founded 2015 · Riverside, CA · California Corporation

Kelker Pharma, Inc. is a California Corporation located in Riverside, CA — just 30 minutes southeast of Los Angeles. We operate state-of-the-art, high-speed automated machinery to produce tablets, capsules, and powders of uncompromised quality — built for brands that refuse to settle.

Our cGMP-certified facility houses nearly 75,000 square feet of manufacturing and warehouse space. Our streamlined operation provides the company with efficiencies that shorten production time without sacrificing quality. Our goal is to provide our customers with the best quality products in a timely manner, while also providing world-class customer service.

Mission

To produce quality products that improve quality of life through better health.

Vision

Continually advance our research and capabilities and apply them to consumer goods we produce — setting the industry standard for innovation and compliance.

Chief Executive Officer

Jim Ayres

"As a contract manufacturer of ingestible products, we all value the level of trust that our customers have in us to support their businesses by producing quality products with outstanding customer service. At Kelker Pharma, you can count on us for responsiveness and products of the highest quality standards."

Jim has over 20 years of experience in the health and wellness industry, including several successful consumer product launches in the mass and specialty retail channels of distribution as well as direct response channels. He has extensive experience in product ideation, applied science, and marketing.

Jim has founded several successful companies and developed nutritional products that have grossed over $100 Million worldwide. Past positions include Executive Vice President of Nova Pharmaceuticals, Inc., VP and Chairman of Gold's Gym Nutrition, VP of Business Development for a global natural product company, and founder and CEO of Innovative Nutrition.
